==========================================================================
                                 Confighp
==========================================================================

You can use the confighp command to customize your hpbar that is displayed
during battle.  Typing 'confighp list' will show you your current hpbar as well
as a list of the variables you can use when setting your hpbar.

confighp help will show you a brief summary of how to use the tool.
confighp default will reset your bar to a sane default.

Note: All variables must be typed in CAPITAL letters.

Examples:

confighp hp HP: $CHP$/$MHP$ SP: $CSP$/$MSP$ W: $CWT$/$MWT$($SWT$) L:
$LMB$%$MPS$ P: $PSN$/$PSR$% T: $TRG$ G: $GXP$

confighp mps A: $MPA$($MPC$)

Last modified: Tue May 20 16:00:04 2014 by Thrax

> confighp
Syntax: confighp [hp|mps|rp|g1|g2] [config line]
        confighp list
        confighp default
> confighp list
Configure HP
============

The list of variables that you can use for confighp are:

$TRG$ + Displays your targets status.
$CHP$ + Displays your current hit points.
$MAS$ - Displays your assault mode.
$CWT$ + Displays your current water.
$GXP$ - Displays your gexp gained last round.
$MPI$ - Displays your pain mode.
$MHP$ - Displays your max hit points.
$BTW$ + Displays your belt water level.
$SWT$ - Displays your current water recirculating in your suit.
$MPC$ - Displays your maula pistol clips.
$MSP$ - Displays your max spell points.
$MPR$ - Displays your prana mode.
$MRE$ - Displays your reflexes mode.
$CRT$ - Carriage Return - Start a new line.
$MBI$ - Displays your bindu mode.
$MWT$ - Displays your max water.
$MPA$ - Displays your maula pistol ammo.
$ATT$ - Displays the number of attackers you are facing.
$LTF$ + Displays your litany focus (full).
$DES$ - Displays minutes until sandtrout reset (or " D" if ready).
$PSN$ - Displays your number of poisons.
$WCR$ - Displays your mode water cost per round.
$LBP$ + Displays your store of pain.
$PSR$ + Displays your poison reset percentage.
$MAT$ - Displays your attacks mode.
$CCP$ + Displays the corpses in your coffin.
$MPS$ - Displays your maula pistol substring when your pistol is drawn.
$LMB$ + Displays your current limberness.
$CSP$ + Displays your current spell points.
$MWE$ - Displays your weirding mode.
$GXA$ - Displays the amount of gxp available to spend.
$MLT$ - Displays your litany mode.
$LTS$ + Displays your litany focus (short).

Entries marked with a + can be set to automatically change colour
depending on their value by adding a + at the beginning, EG: $+CHP$

Angel Help System [Topic: Hpset]
-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=
Category:      General
Syntax:        SYNTAX: hpset <hpbar|gline1|gline2|gp1|gp2> <value|default|show>
-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=
The hpset command allows you to customize your hpbar or the gline1 and
2 variables which appear in Portal (MIPS).  The valid variables are:

$HP$        Current hit points
$HPMAX$     Max hit points
$SP$        Current spell points
$SPMAX$     Max spell points
$TS$        Current time-slices
$TSMAX$     Max time-slices
$TSU$       Time-slices used last round
$TSD$       Time-slice change since last round
$DV$        Current divinity
$DVMAX$     Max divinity
$DVU$       Divinity used last round
$DVD$       Divinity change since last round
$EN$        Current endurance
$ENMAX$     Max endurance
$ADVHO$     Advantage for high outside
$WKHO$      Weakness to high outside
$ADVHI$     Advantage for high inside
$WKHI$      Weakness to high inside
$ADVLO$     Advantage for low outside
$WKLO$      Weakness to low outside
$ADVLI$     Advantage for low inside
$WKLI$      Weakness to low inside
$ENEMY$     Health status of enemy
$DIST$      Distance between player and mob
$NL$        Forced new line
$FLAGS$     Indicates what powers are active (wings, aegis, etc)
$FOCUSSTAT$ Currently focused skill
$FOCUSGXP$  Progress on currently focused skill
$RET$       Retribution ability variable
$COF$       Coffin contents/maximum
$RB$        Rebirths available
$RBM$       Max rebirths
$RBR$       Rebirths reset
$YIN$       Current Yin value
$YANG$      Current Yang value
$YANGMAX$   Max Yang value


You can preview your current settings by using "show", or reset the
values back to "default".

hpset hpbar H: $HP$/$HPMAX$ S: $SP$/$SPMAX$ T: $TS$/$TSMAX$($TSU$) D: $DV$/$DVMAX$ E: $EN$/$ENMAX$ R: $RET$ En: $ENEMY$ $NL$Di: $DIST$ F: $FLAGS$ Yi: $YIN$ Ya: $YANG$/$YANGMAX$ RB: $RB$/$RBM$ ($RBR$%) $NL$HO: $ADVHO$/$WKHO$ HI: $ADVHI$/$WKHI$ LO: $ADVLO$/$WKLO$ LI: $ADVLI$/$WKLI$ G: $FOCUSGXP$
